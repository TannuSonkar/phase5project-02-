In 2015, Phase 5 was created. A digital communication agency focused on the world of health and institutions. Armed with their experience, the founders wanted to gradually expand to other universes and had two aims in mind: constantly explore all the experience possibilities in order to create new experiences, and build up client relationships that would be closer and more intimate.

Phase 5 thus became PHA5E, a studio whose DNA is based on offering a constantly renewed perspective with the capacity to rigorously support you and surprise you.
